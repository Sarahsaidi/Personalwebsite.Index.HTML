In this website, I will tell a little bit about my big family. Why do I say that my family is a big family because every time Hari Raya Aidifitri arrives, my cousins who live far away such as in Johor, Melaka and Negeri Sembilan they will return to their hometown where I and my grandmother live together in Shah Alam. Previously, our country was hit by the Covid-19 virus for two and a half years and due to the virus, we could not gather on Hari Raya Aidilfitri due to the virus. But in 2022, thank God all of my cousins can return to Shah Alam and celebrate Hari Raya Aidilfitri together. Next, my big family will hold various programs at the end of the year. For example, at the end of 2016, we held a family day in Melaka. various activities that we do such as walking around the city of Melaka and at night we have bbq activities. Apart from doing family day, my big family also has a bowling competition at the end of the year. Other than that, we as a big family also do various activities if all the cousins gather such as watching movies, walking around Kuala Lumpur and many more.
